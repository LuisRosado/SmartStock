Conversational Retail Inventory Replenishment Assistant 

PROBLEM STATEMENT 
Retail managers and supply chain teams often struggle with timely and accurate inventory replenishment decisions 
due to fragmented data sources and lack of intuitive decision support. Existing inventory management systems provide 
raw stock levels and sales data but lack interactive, context-aware tools that guide reorder recommendations. 
Overstocking leads to increased holding costs and waste, while stockouts cause lost sales and dissatisfied customers. 
The decision-making process is manual, time-consuming, and error-prone, especially in fast-moving consumer goods 
environments with thousands of SKUs. Moreover, collaboration between store managers, suppliers, and procurement 
teams is hindered by inconsistent information sharing. There is a pressing need for an AI assistant that can understand 
inventory status, forecast immediate replenishment needs, and interact conversationally to help staff quickly resolve 
inventory gaps. 

Data Considerations: 
SKU-level inventory stock counts and sales transaction data in structured formats (JSON, CSV) from internal retail 
systems or simulated APIs. Product metadata including supplier details and reorder lead times. External demand 
signals from promotional calendars or market trends if available. Data volume should represent typical store-level 
inventory units (hundreds to thousands of SKUs). Data cleaning includes normalization of product codes and 
timestamp alignment. Privacy concerns focus on masking store identifiers and sensitive supplier contracts. Synthetic 
datasets with varied demand and supply scenarios can be generated for testing. 
Consider using synthetic or anonymized data where appropriate. Ensure data quality and relevance to the problem 
context. 

SOLUTION EXPECTATIONS 
An interactive web-based chatbot interface where retail staff input queries about inventory levels and receive AI
generated reorder recommendations with rationale. Features include conversational querying, real-time inventory 
status display, reorder alerts, and supplier contact integration. The AI agent uses natural language understanding and 
retrieval augmented generation to process inventory data and generate clear, actionable responses. Integration with 
inventory management and sales APIs or mock data feeds ensures realistic testing. Success will be measured by 
recommendation accuracy, response speed, and positive user feedback during demo. 
Feel free to explore creative solutions that align with the problem context and objectives.
